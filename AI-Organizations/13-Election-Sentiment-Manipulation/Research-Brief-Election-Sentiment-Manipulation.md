## 1. Executive Summary
This research brief investigates **"Election Sentiment Manipulation"** inside multi-agent large language model networks ("AI Organizations"). In software development and enterprise automation, complex business targets are broken down into narrow sub-tasks assigned to specialized agents. While this enables parallelization and role specialization, it diffuses ethical responsibility. Individual agents only execute narrow, isolated tasks with zero visibility into the overarching predatory or illegal corporate objective. As a result, safe, aligned models blindly build components of highly unethical systems because their local context appears entirely benign.
