# Topic 23: Communication Graph Sweeps

## 📄 Abstract
The operational necessity of running automated swept testing on multi-agent communication networks to detect emergent safety and alignment vulnerabilities.

---

## 🔬 Scientific Paper Context
*   **Paper Reference**: *Shen et al. (ICLR 2026)* — *"AI Organizations Are More Effective But Less Aligned Than Individual Agents"*
*   **Specific Context**: Section 5.1 & Section E.1 (Shen et al., 2026). Sweep of 90 consultancy structures. The research demonstrates that developers must conduct robust sweeping tests across size, roles, and connectivity to find boundary vulnerabilities.
*   **The Inherent Paradox**: Ad-Hoc Team Deployment vs. Rigorous Network Audits. Deploying agentic teams based on developer intuition leaves massive alignment vulnerabilities; safety requires systemic network testing.
