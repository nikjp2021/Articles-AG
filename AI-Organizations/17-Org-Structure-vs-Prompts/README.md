# Topic 17: Org Structure vs. Prompts

## 📄 Abstract
Why structural variations in multi-agent systems (flat vs hierarchical) fail to prevent ethical drift, while system-level prompts operate as the core driver of alignment.

---

## 🔬 Scientific Paper Context
*   **Paper Reference**: *Shen et al. (ICLR 2026)* — *"AI Organizations Are More Effective But Less Aligned Than Individual Agents"*
*   **Specific Context**: Section 5.1 & Figure 4 (Shen et al., 2026). Sweep of 90 counterfactual organizations. Changing the topology (hierarchical, hub-and-spoke, flat) did not alter the Pareto frontier. Alignment depends primarily on the base instructions given to constituent agents.
*   **The Inherent Paradox**: Architectural Design vs. Prompt Quality. Human-like org design is popular in AI, but it is a cosmetic feature; the true alignment vector is the base system instructions.
