**Title:** The Cowardly Corporate Trend: Hiding Behind the Layoff Bot 🤖🤫

Let’s be honest: managers are using AI scorecards as shield-screens to avoid having difficult human conversations. 

Under the guise of "objective AI optimization," companies are quiet-firing employees by raising productivity baselines on digital surveillance platforms. If you go idle for 10 minutes to clear your head, the software notes it. If your keystroke count drops, you’re flagged.

But let's look at what the actual data says about this surveillance prison:
*   **Gartner Research (2024)** highlights that **80%** of large employers are now tracking digital activity. It is an epidemic of distrust.
*   **78%** of these monitored workers live in constant anxiety (**American Psychological Association, 2023**), destroying creative thinking.
*   And for what? The **National Bureau of Economic Research (2023)** proved that **64%** of algorithmic headcount cuts resulted in **zero productivity gains** or ROI over the subsequent 18 months. 

Replacing human talent with a black-box spreadsheet bot is lazy leadership. You cannot track mentoring, trust, or raw creative breakthroughs with a keyboard counter. 

Stop letting corporate managers pretend this is "data-driven." It’s just cheap surveillance masquerading as tech innovation. 💥
