**Title:** Imagine Being Fired by a Robot... 🤖💔

We’ve all heard of the phrase "working for the man," but what happens when "the man" is an invisible computer algorithm? 

Right now, thousands of workers are waking up to a new reality where their daily performance is scored by "bossware" tracking their every keystroke, webcam movement, and chat response. If the score drops below a certain line, the system automatically flags them for termination—no human managers, no empathetic conversations, just an automated email.

It sounds like science fiction, but it is happening right now. 

But does this mechanical approach actually make businesses run better? 🤷‍♀️
*   **Gartner Research (2024)** reports that a staggering **80%** of larger employers are now actively tracking employee digital activities. 
*   However, this constant tracking is driving employees to the brink. An **American Psychological Association (2023)** study shows that **78%** of workers feel constant anxiety about being judged by a rigid algorithm.
*   Worse yet, the **National Bureau of Economic Research (2023)** found that **64%** of companies that used AI to cut headcounts saw **zero ROI** or productivity gains over the next 18 months! 📉

When we reduce people to numbers, we lose the magic that makes creative teams work. You can't track trust, dedication, or mutual support with a keystroke counter.
