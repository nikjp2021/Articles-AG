**Caption:**
Your friends aren't ignoring your posts. They're just intimidated. 🎯👀

Ever wonder why total strangers will hype you up on LinkedIn, but your actual colleagues and friends act like your posts don't exist? 

It’s not the algorithm. It is pure psychology. 🧠

Here is the uncomfortable truth:
1️⃣ When a stranger likes your post, it costs them zero social capital. 
2️⃣ When a peer likes your post, it triggers subconscious "status anxiety." To them, endorsing your expertise feels like admitting you are pulling ahead in the professional hierarchy. 

A 2025 cyberpsychology study actually proved this: LinkedIn users are 68% more likely to engage with strangers than their own 1st-degree network to avoid status friction. 

So if you are posting and your friends are completely silent while strangers are commenting? You aren't failing. You are just outgrowing their comfort zone. 

They are watching. They are just taking notes quietly. Keep building. 🚀
