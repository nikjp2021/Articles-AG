**Caption:**
Ever feel like you are shouting into the void when it comes to your own professional network? 🗣️

It is incredibly common to see massive engagement from complete strangers on LinkedIn, while your actual friends and former colleagues remain totally silent. 

Don't take it personally. It is a well-documented psychological phenomenon called **Status Preservation Bias**. Strangers have nothing to lose by hyping you up, but peers often feel subconscious "status anxiety" when they see you stepping into an authority role. 🧠✨

So if your friends aren't liking your posts... don't stop. It just means you are growing beyond their comfort zone, and trust us, they are still reading every word.
