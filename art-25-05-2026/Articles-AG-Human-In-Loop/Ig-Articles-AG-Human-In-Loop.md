**Caption:**
The biggest mistake you can make with AI is letting it run completely unchecked. 🛑

While the **Articles-AG** multi-agent pipeline is incredibly powerful—capable of researching, writing, generating images, and reviewing high-quality content across 4 platforms—it is built on a core philosophy: **Human-in-the-Loop**. 

By intentionally stopping before the "Publish" button, we ensure that zero fake news and zero wrong narratives ever make it to your audience. The AI does the grueling 95% of the work, but you retain the final 5% of editorial control. 🧠✨

Don't sacrifice your brand's reputation for the sake of convenience.
