**Title:** Automate Content Quality: How the Articles-AG Pipeline Fixes the Marketing Bottleneck 🚀

**Intro:**
Scaling your content shouldn't mean sacrificing quality. With Articles-AG, our 6-step multi-agent AI pipeline delivers perfect posts—reviewed, illustrated, and verified—before you even click publish.

The biggest lie in modern marketing is that to post more, you have to lower your standards. For years, teams have been trapped in a grueling cycle: draft, format, source images, review, edit, and schedule across four different platforms. It leads to burnout and, ultimately, generic filler content.

Enter **Articles-AG**. 

This isn't just another AI writing tool. It is a fully autonomous, multi-agent AI content pipeline. It operates as an entire specialized marketing team working in perfect synchronization:
1. **The Researcher**: Scours the web for viral trends and pulls verifiable data.
2. **The Writer**: Drafts four unique, platform-optimized posts (LinkedIn, Facebook, Threads, Instagram).
3. **The Organizer**: Manages file structures automatically.
4. **The Enricher**: Generates highly specific, platform-tailored physical image assets.
5. **The Reviewer**: Subjects the content to a ruthless 13-point quality rubric (checking hooks, formatting, citations, and even alt-text).

If a draft fails the review, the system logs the mistake and automatically rewrites it. The result? A 400% increase in content output while eliminating review bottlenecks. You get the scale of automation with the quality control of a senior editor.
