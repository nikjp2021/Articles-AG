**Title:** Is AI Creating a Massive New Global Divide? 🌍

Is AI going to leave half the world behind? 🤔🌍

We hear all the time about how Artificial Intelligence is going to change the world and create trillions of dollars in new wealth. But who is actually getting richer? 

According to the **UNCTAD Technology and Innovation Report 2025**, most of that massive wealth is staying in places like the US and China. Meanwhile, countries in the Global South (parts of Africa, Latin America, and Asia) are facing a huge challenge. 

Here is what is really happening:
🔋 **The Infrastructure Gap:** Building AI takes massive data centers and electricity. Many developing nations don't have this infrastructure yet, meaning they have to rely on foreign tech companies just to participate.
🛠️ **Job Shifts:** AI can do amazing things for farming and healthcare, but it also threatens traditional labor-intensive jobs that many of these economies rely on. 
🗣️ **Local Voices:** Most AI is trained on Western data. If AI doesn't understand local languages or cultures, it won't actually help the people living there!

But there’s a silver lining! Local tech heroes in these countries are starting to build their own AI—systems designed by them, for them. They are creating AI that speaks local dialects and solves real, everyday problems in their communities. 💡
