**Title:** Is AI Creating a Massive New Global Divide? 🌍

**Slide 1 — Hook**
**Alt Text:** A globe with a glowing crack separating the Northern and Southern hemispheres.
Is AI Creating a Massive New Global Divide? 🌍
(Who really gets the trillions?) 👀

**Slide 2 — The Promise**
**Alt Text:** A futuristic city covered in neon lights and flying cars.
The $15 Trillion Promise 💸
AI is projected to add over $15 Trillion to the global economy by 2030. 
But according to the **UNCTAD Technology and Innovation Report 2025**, the vast majority of that wealth will stay in the Global North.

**Slide 3 — The Compute Deficit**
**Alt Text:** A giant server farm in a cold, snowy landscape connected by a thin wire to a bustling, warm city.
The Compute Deficit 🔋
To build AI, you need massive data centers, stable power, and fast internet. 
The Global South currently lacks this "compute" infrastructure, forcing a dangerous dependency on foreign tech giants. 📉

**Slide 4 — The Language Problem**
**Alt Text:** A glowing AI brain with English text scrolling around it, ignoring other languages.
The Language Problem 🗣️
Most major AI models are trained almost entirely on Western data and English. 
If an AI doesn't understand local dialects or cultural context, it can't effectively solve local problems. 

**Slide 5 — The Threat to Jobs**
**Alt Text:** A robot arm replacing a factory worker.
The Threat to Jobs 🛠️
While AI boosts productivity, it also threatens labor-intensive industries. 
For many developing economies, this poses a severe risk of job displacement without a safety net. ⚠️

**Slide 6 — Fighting Back**
**Alt Text:** A group of diverse developers collaborating on a laptop in a sunny workspace.
Fighting Back: Local Innovation 💡
It’s not all bad news! Grassroots movements across Africa, Latin America, and Asia are building "decolonized" AI. 
They are creating models trained on indigenous languages and designed to solve regional challenges from the ground up. 🔥

**Slide 7 — CTA**
**Alt Text:** A bright question mark over a diverse crowd.
What do you think? 
How can we make sure AI benefits the whole world, not just a few countries?
Let me know below! 👇💬

**Caption:**
AI is projected to add trillions to the global economy, but who is really cashing the check? For the Global South, the AI boom brings massive potential—and an even bigger risk of a widening technological divide. 🌍📊

While the lack of infrastructure and the concentration of tech power in the Global North present massive hurdles, grassroots innovators across the Global South are pushing back. They are building localized, language-first AI models to ensure their communities aren't left behind. 💡
