# **Threads – The Real Cost of Pushing New Tech Without Training**

**Caption:**
Leaders love the hype of the newest tools, but rolling them out without proper training drags productivity down. 🚀

**Why it happens:**
- Speed beats strategy
- Training budgets get cut
- Teams expected to self‑learn

**The fallout:**
1️⃣ Morale drops – employees feel undervalued.
2️⃣ Productivity dips – troubleshooting replaces output.
3️⃣ Errors multiply – data quality suffers.
4️⃣ Turnover rises – talent leaves for supportive environments.

**A better path:**
- Pilot small, gather feedback.
- Offer structured onboarding & hands‑on labs.
- Build champion networks for peer mentoring.
- Track adoption metrics to prove ROI.

**Bottom line:** Tech is only as powerful as the people who use it. Pair tools with real learning to unlock true innovation.
