**Title:** The Special Rage of Legacy Code 😭🔥

There is a special kind of rage reserved for undocumented legacy code that breaks at 4 PM on a Friday. 😭🔥

1/ 🧵 It's always the system that hasn't been touched in four years. You're just trying to trace one simple error, and suddenly you're three microservices deep into a codebase that looks like it was written in ancient hieroglyphs. 👀

2/ The pressure is the worst part. The business needs it up NOW, but proper root cause analysis isn't a 5-minute fix. (Yes, the 2022 Postman State of the API Report notes that debugging and fixing issues is one of the top time-consuming tasks for devs, and right now I absolutely get it.) 🗣️

3/ But I have to admit... after 5 hours of pulling my hair out, finally finding that one rogue configuration file and seeing the system spin back to life? That feeling is unmatched. ✨💅

4/ Anyway, write your documentation, people. Your future self (and your team) will thank you. 🤝💯 #TechTwitter #Debugging #DevLife
